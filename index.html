    • Pet ID Card

  • Thanks to our card application, vets are able to see recording animals and pets information. The card application provides a lot of convenience to pets owner and vets. If the pet owner is far from his or her clinic he/she can go to any clinic easily. Because thanks to pet/animal card all vets can see pets/animals recording information thus owner do not need to afraid of wrong treatment and madicine. Card includes patient information such as age, gender, race, birth date, allergic reactions, past and current disease, vaccotion informations, medicines and treatment information exc. card also includes owners information.
